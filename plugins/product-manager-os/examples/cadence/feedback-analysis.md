## Top themes in detail

### 1. Reminder/notification fatigue тАФ P0
- **What:** Teams get too many check-in reminders, at the wrong times, and start tuning Slack pings out entirely тАФ which then drives the missed check-ins.
- **Volume / trend:** 41 comments (20%), тЖС +7pp vs. prior period. Climbing every period.
- **Voice of customer:** "We get pinged 3 times before standup time and people have started muting the Cadence channel." тАФ Zendesk #4471, Team plan, 38 seats. / "I'd check in if it nudged me once, at a time that made sense for my timezone, not on a fixed 9am blast." тАФ NPS 4, eng manager.
- **Likely root cause:** Reminder cadence is global and fixed; no per-person quiet hours or escalation logic. Muting is the rational response, and a muted channel is a dead check-in habit.
- **Recommendation:** Ship per-person reminder windows + a "one smart nudge" mode before investing further in Blocker Detector. **Owner:** Eng (notifications) + Design. **When:** now.

### 2. Week-2 habit drop-off / no re-engagement тАФ P0
- **What:** Teams check in enthusiastically in week 1, then go quiet. Nothing in the product re-engages a team that has lapsed.
- **Volume / trend:** 33 comments (16%), тЖС +5pp vs. prior period.
- **Voice of customer:** "Week one everyone posted. Week two it was me and one other person. There's no nudge to the manager that the habit is dying." тАФ NPS 5, team lead, 22 seats. / "Cancelled тАФ we never built the routine and Cadence never told us we were slipping." тАФ churn ticket #4502.
- **Likely root cause:** No lapse detection or manager-facing "your team is slipping" signal; onboarding sells the demo, not the habit. Directly the week-2 retention problem on our roadmap.
- **Recommendation:** Add a manager alert when participation drops >40% week-over-week, plus a lightweight day-8 re-onboarding nudge. **Owner:** PM + Eng. **When:** now.

### 3. Timezone & scheduling rigidity тАФ P1
- **What:** Distributed teams can't get check-in windows that fit multiple regions; one fixed time penalizes someone.
- **Volume / trend:** 28 comments (14%), тЖТ flat.
- **Voice of customer:** "Half my team is in IST. A 9am ET window means they answer at 6:30pm тАФ so they don't." тАФ Zendesk #4488, 51 seats.
- **Likely root cause:** Single team-level schedule with no per-member local-time resolution. Overlaps heavily with theme 1 тАФ fixing reminder windows partly fixes this.
- **Recommendation:** Resolve check-in windows in each member's local timezone. **Owner:** Eng. **When:** watch (bundle with theme 1).
